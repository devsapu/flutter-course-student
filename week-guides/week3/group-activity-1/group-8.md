## Goal

Improve how the todo list looks: **padding**, **icons**, **Card** widgets, or **theme** colors — keep it readable.

---

## Steps

1. Branch: `week3-group-8/ui-polish`

4. Add **empty state** text if list is empty (nice message)
5. Commit, push, PR
